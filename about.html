Traders Training Academy (TTA) is a premier stock market academy in Bangalore, established in 2016 with a mission to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills to navigate the dynamic world of finance. Led by CEO Santhosh Kumar, an NSE Certified Trainer with over 10 years of experience, TTA has transformed the investment journeys of over 15,000 students.

Why Choose

Traders Training Academy?

Excellence and Recognition: TTA is an ISO-certified academy, a testament to our commitment to quality education. In 2024, we were honored with the Excellence Academy Award, a prestigious recognition of our continued dedication to empowering students.
Experienced Mentorship: Our mentor, with his 15+ years of expertise, guides students through the intricacies of the stock market. NSE certification ensures you receive training aligned with industry standards.
Comprehensive Curriculum: Our meticulously designed curriculum caters to beginners and seasoned individuals alike. We offer a range of courses, from fundamental and technical analysis to advanced futures and options strategies, equipping you with over 100 powerful trading techniques.
Unmatched Support: We understand the importance of practical experience. TTA provides unparalleled support, including a staggering of up to 200 days of live trading assistance under the guidance of our expert trainers. This ensures you gain the confidence to navigate real-world market scenarios.
Flexible Learning Options: At TTA, we cater to diverse learning styles. Choose between offline, online, or a blended learning approach to suit your schedule and preferences. Weekend classes are available for those with busy weekdays.
Focus on Practical Application: We don't just teach theory. Our curriculum emphasizes practical application, ensuring you graduate with the skills to make informed investment decisions. Exposure to real-time market situations through live trading sessions further equips you for success.
